# My Profile Management

You can view and modify information for the current account. Both Root and Member accounts can access My Information management.

## View and Modify My Account Information

You can view and modify detailed information for your own account.

The items that can be viewed are as follows.

- ID, Name, Role, Email
- Granted DB service permissions
- Account status, creation date, last login date, modification date

To modify, **[Edit]** Click the button.
