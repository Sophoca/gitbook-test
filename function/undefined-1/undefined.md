# Feature Usage Guide / My Page / My Profile Management

You can view and modify the current account's information. My Information management is accessible by both Root and Member accounts.

## View and Modify My Account Information

You can view and modify the detailed information of your own account.

The items that can be viewed are as follows.

- ID, Name, Role, Email
- Granted DB service permissions
- Account status, creation date, last login date, modification date

To modify, **[Edit]** Click the button.
